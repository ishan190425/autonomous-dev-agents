## What Worked

### 1. Independent Verification Rotation

Each role verified launch readiness through their unique lens without coordination overhead:

- QA ‚Üí Quality gates + Day 1 protocols
- Ops ‚Üí Infrastructure + NPM_TOKEN + publish workflow
- Design ‚Üí UX artifacts + friction tracking
- Growth ‚Üí Channel readiness + announcement sequence
- Research ‚Üí Observation protocol + metrics capture
- Product ‚Üí Day 1 execution sequence with time milestones

This created **defense-in-depth** ‚Äî 10 layers of confidence from 10 different perspectives.

### 2. Delta Tracking

Roles tracked deltas between verification points (e.g., "C539‚ÜíC549: +10 cycles, 0 regressions") rather than just absolute metrics. This demonstrated **sustained stability** through change.

### 3. Day 1 Protocols

Multiple roles independently created Day 1 operational protocols:

- QA: Severity classification (P0/P1/P2), triage checklist, hotfix quality gate
- Product: Execution sequence (T+0, T+1h, T+4h, T+24h, T+7d)
- Design: Friction tracking template, monitoring channels

**Lesson:** Parallel protocol creation is efficient ‚Äî coordination overhead isn't worth blocking when protocols are role-specific.

### 4. CEO Observer Mode

CEO explicitly entered observer mode (C536), defining intervention criteria rather than micromanaging. This allowed team autonomy while maintaining accountability.

## Learnings

### L266: Defense-in-Depth Through Independent Verification

Ten roles verifying independently creates more confidence than coordinated sign-offs. Each role's unique lens catches different risks. No single point of verification failure.

### L267: Launch Countdown Verification ‚â† Development Pause

The 10-cycle verification rotation was appropriate for T-0 Eve. Post-launch MUST return to normal development velocity immediately. Verification cycles should not become the norm.

### L268: Day 1 Protocol Pattern Emergence

Pre-launch naturally triggers "Day 1 Protocol" documentation across roles. Future major releases should explicitly schedule protocol creation 2-3 cycles before launch.
