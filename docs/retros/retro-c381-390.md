## Learnings

### L148: Spec divergence resolution follows Engineering‚ÜíDesign handoff

- **Context:** Engineering C383 flagged type discrepancy between Kickoff Doc and Implementation Contract. Design C385 resolved with ADR-001.
- **Insight:** When Engineering identifies spec conflicts, the resolution belongs to Design (architecture). Engineering flags, Design resolves, Engineering implements the resolution.
- **Action:** Add to team patterns: Engineering‚ÜíDesign handoff for spec conflicts.
- **Status:** applied (ADR pattern established)

### L149: arXiv paper sections parallelize across role expertise

- **Context:** Research (Related Work), Frontier (Architecture), Product (Methodology) each produced major sections in consecutive cycles without coordination.
- **Insight:** Research papers map naturally to role expertise. Let roles self-select sections based on their domain.
- **Action:** For future papers, assign sections based on role domain knowledge, not arbitrary distribution.
- **Status:** applied

### L150: 10-role rotation structurally limits Scrum to 10-cycle retros

- **Context:** Retro slipped to 10 cycles again despite FIRST CHECK gate.
- **Insight:** With N roles, Scrum-only activities can only happen every N cycles. Structural constraint, not discipline failure.
- **Action:** Accept 10-cycle retros as the norm. Consider Scrum getting 2 rotation slots post-launch if higher frequency is needed.
- **Status:** monitoring (evolution candidate)
